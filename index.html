Welcome to the first
sports investing platform!

We have developed an investment platform that allows our users to gain profit from special sports tokens, the value of which depends on the performance of individual athletes or sports clubs.

How does it work?

The platform allows you to multiply your funds as the values of your favourite teams and players increase. No sweepstakes or bets involved.

How is it possible?

We released internal tokens for every professional athlete/sports club on a platform with token price based on publicly available ratings.

How to earn?

Our platform allows users to purchase a token assigned to any athlete or club at the SIT market value and sell it when the market value rises.

Making money on sports without betting. Our coin for sports fans, analysts and investors.

The way it works is really simple.

Step by step

How does our platform work?

Step 1.

Buy an SIT

Purchase SIT on a decentralised crypto-exchange in order to use them on the Sport Investing platform.

Step 2.

Choose a promising player or a club for investment

Register and choose an athlete or a club or consult experienced analysts on our analytical portal when choosing a token.

Step 3.

Purchase a token

Invest in the token assigned to an athlete or a club with SIT according to the current market price based on a publicly available source.

Step 4.

Track the value of the purchased tokenised assets

Monitor the change in the market price of the tokenised athlete or club that you have purchased on the Sport Investing platform and receive an email notification when the price starts to rise sharply.

Step 5.

Sell the tokens

When the market value of the acquired tokenised assets assigned to a player or a club rises, you can sell them and receive SIT tokens at a current cost.

Step 6.

Get a profit

You can exchange SIT tokens for any other cryptocurrency on the market or purchase new tokenised shares assigned to other players or clubs on the platform

SIT TOKENS.MULTI-CHAIN.LISTING

What is a SIT?

In order to buy athletes'/clubs' tokens on the platform, users deal with SIT, which can be traded on crypto-exchanges. The token is launched on the Binance Smart Chain (BEP20) and has already been verified by the Binance Smart Chain Explorer validator.

In the third quarter of 2021, the token will become a multi-chain and will be available in the Freeton, Ethereum, Tron blockchains, using the development of our SIT connecting bridge team.

Date of listing on a decentralized exchange: 15.08.2021
